### Classpects at ICSE 2005

Our paper, Classpects: Unifying Aspect- and Object-Oriented Language Design, was presented at ICSE 2005. It unifies aspects and objects into a single construct, the classpect, which improves the conceptual integrity of the language and how aspect modules compose.
