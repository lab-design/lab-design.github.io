---
title: "NSF EAGER seeds Boa, a community infrastructure for mining software repositories"
---

An early NSF EAGER award seeded Boa, the lab's infrastructure for mining software repositories at scale. The grant supported the first version of a platform that lets a researcher write an analysis once and run it across very large collections of code.

That seed grew into a community resource used in software-engineering research and into later, larger awards that expanded the infrastructure.

This work is part of [Analyzing Software at Scale, with Boa](/research/#software-at-scale); see [our overview of reading the world's code at once](/news/2025/12/10/analyzing-software-at-scale-with-boa/). Details are on the [NSF award page](https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=1349153).
