---
title: "NSF HDR TRIPODS funds the D4 Institute for dependable data science"
---

The National Science Foundation, through its Harnessing the Data Revolution program, has funded the D4 (Dependable Data Driven Discovery) Institute, with Hridesh Rajan among its leaders. The institute joins the theoretical foundations of data science with the software-engineering practices needed to make data-driven systems dependable.

Its work spans the data-science lifecycle, from the data and the models to the pipelines that connect them, with the aim of reducing the risks that arise when decisions are driven by data. Much of the lab's research on the dependability of machine learning grew out of this effort.

This work is part of [Modular and Dependable AI](/research/#modular-and-dependable-ai). Details are on the [project page](http://tads.research.iastate.edu/).
