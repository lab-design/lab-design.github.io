---
title: "NSF supports the JML community infrastructure for formal methods"
---

The National Science Foundation is supporting a collaborative effort to revitalize the tools and documentation behind the Java Modeling Language (JML), a notation for writing precise specifications of Java programs that formal-methods researchers rely on.

The award sustains shared infrastructure so that the broader community can build on a common, well-maintained foundation.

This work is part of [Modularity and Modular Reasoning](/research/#modularity-and-modular-reasoning). Details are on the [NSF award page](https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=0709217).
