### Elected a Fellow of the AAAS

Hridesh Rajan has been elected a Fellow of the American Association for the Advancement of Science, one of the most distinctive honors in the scientific community. The honor recognizes his contributions to data-driven science, in particular to modularity and modular reasoning in software and to the development of the Boa language and infrastructure.
