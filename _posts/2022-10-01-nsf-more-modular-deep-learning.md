---
title: "NSF supports More Modular Deep Learning"
---

The National Science Foundation is supporting the lab's project on more modular deep learning. The work builds on our finding that a trained neural network can be decomposed into modules, and asks how to make those modules more general, more reliable, and easier to reuse and replace.

Modularity offers a path to building and updating models at lower cost and energy, and to repairing them by replacing the part responsible for a problem rather than retraining the whole network.

This work is part of [Modular and Dependable AI](/research/#modular-and-dependable-ai); see [our modular deep learning overview](/news/2026/04/15/modular-deep-learning/). Details are on the [NSF award page](https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=2223812).
