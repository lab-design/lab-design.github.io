### Ptolemy at ECOOP 2008

Our paper, Ptolemy: A Language with Quantified, Typed Events, was presented at ECOOP 2008. Ptolemy uses quantified, typed events as an interface between modules, with translucid contracts that support modular reasoning about implicit invocation.
