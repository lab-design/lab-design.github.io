Congratulations to **Ruchira Manke**, whose paper "AgentInspect: Diagnosing Behavioral Failures in Artificial Intelligence Agents" has been accepted at the 35th ACM SIGSOFT International Symposium on Software Testing and Analysis (ISSTA 2026). The work introduces a coverage-guided framework that detects six categories of behavioral failures in LangChain-based AI agents, and shows that simulating abnormal tool behavior surfaces failures ordinary testing misses. Congratulations as well to her collaborators Mohammad Wardat (Oakland University), Foutse Khomh (Polytechnique Montréal), and Hridesh Rajan (Tulane University).
