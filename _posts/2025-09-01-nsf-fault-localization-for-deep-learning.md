---
title: "NSF funds Fault Localization for Deep Learning"
---

The National Science Foundation is supporting the lab's project on fault localization for deep learning, a collaboration with Mohammad Wardat at Oakland University. The work continues a line of research on finding, explaining, and repairing faults in deep learning systems.

The project aims to make it routine for the people who build deep learning to locate the cause of a model's failure and act on it, the way developers of ordinary software already can.

This work is part of [Modular and Dependable AI](/research/#modular-and-dependable-ai); see our overview of [debugging deep learning](/news/2025/10/15/why-deep-learning-needs-debugging-tools/). Details are on the [NSF award page](https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=2419882).
