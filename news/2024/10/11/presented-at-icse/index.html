Three Lab Members Presented at ICSE 2025

October 11, 2024

Three members of our lab presented their work at ICSE’25 in Ottawa, Canada:

  • Deepak-George Thomas presented his paper titled “µPRL: A Mutation Testing Pipeline for Deep Reinforcement Learning based on Real Faults” (link: paper).
  • Fraol Batole presented his paper titled “An LLM-Based Agent-Oriented Approach for Automated Code Design Issue Localization” (link: paper).
  • Ruchira Manke presented her paper titled “Mock Deep Testing: Toward Separate Development of Data and Models for Deep Learning” (link: paper).
